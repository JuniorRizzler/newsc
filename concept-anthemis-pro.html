Concept Page

Anthemis Pro

A professional-brilliance design direction built around monograph styling, asymmetrical editorial layouts, and oceanic turquoise balanced by forged orange accents.

Core Intent

This concept rejects standard SaaS visual language in favor of a digital publication feel: layered vellum-like surfaces, oversized editorial headers, and almost invisible boundaries created through tonal hierarchy.

Primary

Turquoise Ink

Accent

Burnt Orange

No-Line Rule

Sections are separated by shifts from `surface` to `surface-container-low`, never by rigid 1px borders.

Editorial Typography

Plus Jakarta Sans drives every scale, with large headlines used as structural anchors instead of decorative flourishes.

Atmospheric Depth

Cards should feel stacked and luminous through tonal lift and ambient shadows rather than standard UI elevation.
