Modern Archivist

This direction is still academic, but with more visible energy. Large headlines, high-contrast asymmetry, and tonal sheets create a curated publication feel rather than a neutral dashboard.

Interaction Language

Buttons and lists should feel more kinetic, with stronger color contrast and more noticeable hover states, while still honoring the no-line boundary rule.

Spatial Rule

Use surface shifts, overlap, and generous spacing to create structure. The design should breathe like a premium journal rather than a cramped utility panel.
