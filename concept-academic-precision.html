Creative North Star

Soul Concept. A premium editorial study platform with refined tonal layering, deliberate asymmetry, and a gallery-like presentation of knowledge rather than generic app chrome.

System Rules

  • No-line separation using whitespace and tonal sections.
  • Large editorial display typography over compact UI labels.
  • Glassmorphism only for floating layers like sticky headers.
  • Depth through layered surfaces, not heavy borders.

Component Guidance

Buttons: primary actions use a deep emerald gradient, secondary buttons use lifted paper surfaces, tertiary actions remain text-forward.

Cards: use nested surface layers with generous padding instead of divider lines.

Inputs: soft-fill fields with ghost borders and subtle focus glow.

Layout: asymmetry and open margins should feel like a monograph, not a dashboard template.
